ADK Automotive Sponsorship
ADK Automotive strives to serve with excellence, serving our valued clients and participants with fantastic automotive events. By sponsoring our events, you are presenting your company to a network of thousands of enthusiasts & potential clients from the Tri-State Region. Being apart of our events will also bring you more brand awareness and recognition to the general public. Our official event sponsors drive the financial aspect to all ADK Automotive Events, allowing us to invest the money to throw fantastic events throughout the year!
OVER 10,000 ACTIVE FOLLOWERS ON SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORMS & GROWING
OVER 8,000 EVENT PARTICIPANTS IN ATTENDANCE IN 2024
OFFERING VARIOUS METHODS OF COMPANY PROMOTION
BENEFITS OF SPONSORSHIP:
Sell Products/Services:
Official Event Sponsors will have the opportunity to display & sell products/services at our events! Seasonal Sponsors will have the opportunity at every event hosted in 2025, Single Day Sponsors are only permitted to display per event purchased.
ADK Promotional Merchandise
At each ADK Automotive Event, our staff will be handing out one “Thank you” pamphlet for participating in the event. The back will showcase all Single Day & Seasonal Sponsor’s logos as well as all for that specific event.
Social Media Promotion:
Social Media drives our events and our following, and it serves as one of our best avenues for sponsorship promotion. SEASONAL & Single Day Sponsors will be promoted Per-Event hosted, as it is when our page receives the most engagement from followers! Additionally, Seasonal Sponsors will receive the benefit of having their content reposted on ADK Social Media periodically throughout the show season.
In Person Event Promotion:
Seasonal Sponsors’ Logos will be featured on our “Official Sponsor” Banner, which is placed outside each of our events towards the crowd for maximum visibility. Single Day and Seasonal Sponsors will be announced via loud speaker during thank you announcement at each 2025 event.
Website Promotion
Our home website, adkautomotive.com serves as the HUB for our events. It allows viewers to view our 2025 event calendar schedule, a place to view past events, and the HUB to purchase tickets for our future events. It serves as a place for clients to learn more about our official SEASONAL sponsors.
ADK Promotional Merchandise
Seasonal Sponsors will be featured on ADK Automotive Merchandise, which will be sold at each event throughout 2025. This includes Hoodies, and T-Shirts.
