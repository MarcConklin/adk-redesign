Morgantown Supercar Show by ADK Automotive
Saturday, November 2, 2024
9:00 AM
1:00 PM
Google Calendar
ICS
Ladies and gentlemen, mark your calendars! Our team is excited to invite you to our next event, the Morgantown Supercar show hosted at
@theclassicautomall
on Saturday, November 2nd. This event is from 9:00am to 1:00pm. Registration is free for both vehicle owners and the general public.
Join hundreds of other enthusiasts at the Classic Automall in Morgantown, PA for a spectacular morning show. The Classic Automall’s facility will be open to the public, with a fantastic display of antique / classic vehicles. Our good friends at
@thehoustoncafe
will be also present at our event with their food truck,
@thehoustonhitch
! Treat yourself to their delicious breakfast / lunch meals during the duration of our show.
This event will be monitored by law enforcement, who will enforce the law if necessary. Please be respectful of event property, (excessive revving, burnouts, reckless behavior) are not tolerated at this event, and will result in banned from all of our events indefinitely.
If you are interested in sponsoring our upcoming events, please submit an application via adkautomotive.com/sponsorship, or email us at info@adkautomotive.com.
