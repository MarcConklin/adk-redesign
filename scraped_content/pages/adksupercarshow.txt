ADK SUPERCAR SHOW
ADK Automotive is excited to present the ADK Supercar Show, hosted twice in 2025 at the iconic Classic Auto Mall in Morgantown, PA, conveniently located just off the PA Turnpike. This premier event brings together an impressive collection of high-end show cars, alongside Classic Auto Malls renowned display of classic vehicles. With engaging activities, a variety of vendors, and delicious food options, the ADK Supercar Show promises to deliver a truly unique and unforgettable experience.

SUPERCARS AT SUNSET
ADK Automotive is thrilled to present Supercars at Sunset, our most anticipated and spectacular event of the year. As the name suggests, this exciting family-friendly event takes place in the evening, offering the perfect backdrop to admire some of the most stunning supercars in the world as the sun sets. Supercars at Sunset is more than just a car show—it’s a celebration for the entire family! Enjoy a variety of activities designed to entertain all ages, from interactive games for kids to engaging experiences for car enthusiasts. We’ve also planned plenty of food vendors offering delicious eats to keep you fueled throughout the evening. And as the event culminates, we’re capping off the night with a breathtaking fireworks display, lighting up the sky and creating an unforgettable finale.
This year, we’re excited to be hosting not one, but two Supercars at Sunset events, ensuring even more opportunities for everyone to join in the fun! Whether you’re a car enthusiast, a family looking for an exciting evening out, or just someone who enjoys a spectacular event, there’s something for everyone at Supercars at Sunset. Learn more about the show and get ready for an evening full of thrills, excitement, and memories that will last a lifetime!
