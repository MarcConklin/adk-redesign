ADK Road Rally November
Sunday, November 26, 2023
2:00 PM
6:30 PM
Google Calendar
ICS
Join us on Sunday, November 26th for our third and final official ADK Road Rally of the year, from Strasburg to the Houston Cafe in Gap, PA!
This road rally features a tasteful mixture of driving roads in the Susquehanna Valley region, including engaging mountain passes, scenic Amish countryside backroads, and open highway driving.
The start point of the drive will begin at Strasburg Mennonite Church, in Strasburg PA at 2:00 pm, with a brief meet & greet car show & food & drinks for our participants to enjoy. After a brief driver meeting at 2:45 pm, our Official Event Pace Vehicles will lead you on a 2-hour journey to our End Destination, located at the Houston Cafe Gap, PA. We will arrive at our end destination at approximately 5:00 pm.
Our end destination will be located at The Houston Cafe in Gap PA, where we will be hosting a complimentary Private Lunch Meal for all of our registered event participants!
