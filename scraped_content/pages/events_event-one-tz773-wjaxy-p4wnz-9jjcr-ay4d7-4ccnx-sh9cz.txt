Houston Cars and Coffee Season Opener
Saturday, April 6, 2024
9:00 AM
12:00 PM
The Houston Co Cafe
835 Houston Run Drive
Gap, PA, 17527
United States
(map)
Google Calendar
ICS
Join us on Saturday, April 6th for our Season Opener Houston Cars and Coffee show; a great event with fellowship, cars, and fantastic food! The Houston Cafe, located just outside of Lancaster PA, provides food and beverage for purchase that you can enjoy while admiring a great variety of high-end automobiles! All makes & models are permitted to attend, no admission fee is required for spectators or event participants. Keep an eye on our social media pages for more event information!
