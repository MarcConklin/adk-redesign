Supercars at Sunset 4th of July Celebration
Saturday, July 6, 2024
6:00 PM
10:00 PM
The Houston Co Cafe
835 Houston Run Drive
Gap, PA, 17527
United States
(map)
Google Calendar
ICS
Join us for our 4th of July celebration on Saturday, July 6th! Our team at ADK Automotive & The Houston Cafe is once again hosting our beloved night event; and this time it’s to celebrate July 4th! Come out with any make & model vehicle and enjoy delicious food & beverages, music, and a fantastic display of rare & high tier automobiles! In addition, we will have games and activities for all ages at this event, including rides, bouncy castle, yard games, axe throwing, and much more. And best of all - an incredible Fireworks Display towards the end of the event!
