ADK Road Rally November
Sunday, November 26, 2023
2:00 PM
6:30 PM

Join us on Sunday, November 26th for our third and final official ADK Road Rally of the year, from Strasburg to the Houston Cafe in Gap, PA!
This road rally features a tasteful mixture of driving roads in the Susquehanna Valley region, including engaging mountain passes, scenic Amish countryside backroads, and open highway driving.
The start point of the drive will begin at Strasburg Mennonite Church, in Strasburg PA at 2:00 pm, with a brief meet & greet car show & food & drinks for our participants to enjoy. After a brief driver meeting at 2:45 pm, our Official Event Pace Vehicles will lead you on a 2-hour journey to our End Destination, located at the Houston Cafe Gap, PA. We will arrive at our end destination at approximately 5:00 pm.
Our end destination will be located at The Houston Cafe in Gap PA, where we will be hosting a complimentary Private Lunch Meal for all of our registered event participants!

Ladies and Gentlemen, we're extremely excited to present to you our first ever
"Supercars at Sunset" event, coming up on
Saturday, August 12th!
Unlike any of our past events - this show will be held at dusk, from 7:00pm to 10:00pm. The event will be held at our Cars and Coffee location, @thehoustoncafe! Our team here at @adk_automotive has highly anticipated this event for quite some time, and we can't wait to have you all! Stay tuned for more information on the event in the coming days. We look forward to seeing you all on the 12th!

Join us on Sunday, June 4th for our first official ADK Road Rally of the year, from Lancaster to Hershey, PA!
This road rally features a tasteful mixture of driving roads in the Susquehanna Valley region, including engaging mountain passes, scenic Amish countryside backroads, and open highway driving.
The start point of the drive will begin in Lancaster PA at 8:00 am, with a brief meet & greet car show & with food & drinks for our participants to enjoy. After a brief drivers meeting at 9:15am, our Official Event Pace Vehicles will lead you on a 2-hour journey, taking you north to our checkpoint located in Pine Grove, then continuing the second leg of the drive to our End Destination, located in Hershey, PA. We will arrive at our end destination at approximately 11:45 am.
Our end destination will be located at The Mill in Hershey, where we will be hosting a complimentary Private Lunch Meal for all of our registered event participants!
